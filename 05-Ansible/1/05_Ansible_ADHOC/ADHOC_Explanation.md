## WHAT's ADHOC COMMAND  ##

  ` Command Without Required PlayBook ``


## WHEN USED ADHOC ##

 ` For Quick Tasks And When  We Used For (1)Time  `
 ` TroubleShooting & Testing  `
 ` Quick Changes  `


## Diffrence Between ADHOC & PlayBook  ##

 ` ADHOC: FAST , SIMPLE , (1)Time  `
 ` PlayBook: MultiTask , NotEasy , Used Again  `

## ADHOC COMMAND STRUCTURAL ##

 ` ansible <hosts> -m <module> -a "<arguments>" [options] `
 ` special Host  OR group ،<hosts>: target hosts all `
 ` -m <modu e>: For used  Module  `
 ` -a "<arguments>": arguments For Used Modules `
 ` Options: -i inventory ،-u user ،--become sudo  `
